Decoction Vamana

Decoction Vamana

Process: Herbal decoction is ingested to produce the action of vomiting after oleation and sweating.

Benefits: It expels the mucus and clears respiratory and gastric congestion, preventing recurrent allergies.

Milk-based Vamana

Milk-based Vamana

Process: Herbal emetics are mixed in milk and given for gentle cleansing.

Benefits: It is good for weak or delicate patients in balancing Kapha without any harshness.

Ghee-based Vamana

Ghee-based Vamana

Process: Medicated ghee is administered with emesis for internal function of the body.

Benefits: It nourishes tissues by removing the toxins, suitable for chronic disorders of Kapha.

Snigdha Virechana

Snigdha Virechana

Process: Laxatives of oily or ghee type are given orally.

Benefits: Cleanses the bowels in a benign way without drying them; good for dry constitutions; relieves acidity.

Ruksha Virechana

Ruksha Virechana

Process: Purgatives are given which are dry and hot in nature, thereby inducing forcible evacuation.

Benefits:This procedure eliminates excess pitta and cures conditions of skin rashes, fevers, jaundice, and burning sensations.

Niruha Basti

Niruha Basti

Process: A powerful enema composed of decoction, honey, ghee, and rock salt.

Benefits: Strong detox, removes Vata/Kapha type of toxins, relieves constipation, bloating, arthritis.

Anuvasana Basti

Anuvasana Basti

Process: Warm medicated oils are to be administered into the rectum after meals.

Benefits: Oils lubricate the colon, nourish tissues, and dispel dryness, weakness, and Vata stiffness.

Matra Basti

Matra Basti

Process: This Basti involves giving a small quantity of oil enema daily and can be administered to all age groups.

Benefits: Very light type of Basti, has immunity and digestion level, prevents recurrence of Vata disorders.

Yapana Basti

Yapana Basti

Process: A nourishing enema made of milk, ghee, honey, and herbal decoction.

Benefits: Strengthens body, improves fertility, vitality, and general weakness.

Lekhana Basti

Lekhana Basti

Ksheera Basti

Ksheera Basti

Process: Medicated milk enema infused with herbs.

Benefits: Cooling, soothing for Pitta disorders, useful in bleeding and burning conditions.

Utkleshana Basti

Utkleshana Basti

Process: Herbs that provoke toxins are given through enema.

Benefits: Mobilizes deeply lodged doshas into the gut for elimination.

Sthambhana Basti

Sthambhana Basti

Process: Astringent/binding decoctions introduced rectally.

Benefits: Stops diarrhea, excessive bowel movements, stabilizes intestinal weakness.

Avapeedaka Nasya

Avapeedaka Nasya

Brimhana Nasya

Brimhana Nasya

Shodhana Nasya

Shodhana Nasya

Siravyadha
