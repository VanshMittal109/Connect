Bashpa Sweda

Bashpa Sweda

Process: Patient sits in a wooden chamber where herbal steam is applied to the entire body, inducing deep sweating.

Benefits: Detoxification, improved circulation, relief from muscle stiffness, and joint pain relief.

Duration: 15-20 minutes per session

Precautions: Not recommended for pregnant women, people with high blood pressure, or those with heart conditions.

Abhyanga

Abhyanga

Process: Full-body warm oil massage using long, sweeping strokes with medicated oils.

Benefits: Improves circulation, enhances muscle tone, rejuvenates the body, and promotes better sleep.

Duration: 45-60 minutes per session

Precautions: Avoid heavy meals before the session. Inform the therapist about any skin conditions.

Shirodhara

Shirodhara

Process: Warm medicated oil is poured in a continuous stream on the forehead (third eye area) for a specific duration.

Benefits: Calms the mind, improves sleep quality, reduces stress and anxiety, and enhances mental clarity.

Duration: 30-45 minutes per session

Precautions: Avoid if you have open wounds on the forehead or severe neck issues.

Nasya

Nasya

Process: Administration of herbal oils or powders through the nasal passages followed by gentle face and neck massage.

Benefits: Cleanses sinuses, improves respiratory health, enhances mental clarity, and relieves headaches.

Duration: 15-20 minutes per session

Precautions: Not recommended during pregnancy, menstruation, or immediately after meals.
